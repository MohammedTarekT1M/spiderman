Spider-Man Miles Morales

Miles Morales is Marvel's newest New York Spider-Man. After his father's death, Miles was introduced to Peter Parker, who quickly became his friend and mentor. When Miles was bitten by a genetically engineered spider, he developed unique powers and, after months of insistence, Peter agreed to train him.
